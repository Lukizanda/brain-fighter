VARIANT — Reservoir-cast active

Player drags from the Green reservoir inward (toward the screen center); a vertical menu opens to the left, listing all currently-affordable tiers of green. With Green at 16: Mend (T1·5) and Stone Wall (T2·10) are bright and selectable; Sanctuary (T3·20) is locked. Finger hovers on Stone Wall — releasing here fires it and drains 10 from Green; the ✛ marks it as a placement spell, so a second aim step will follow.
Release inside the dashed cancel zone (anywhere off the menu) = cancel — no energy spent. A simple tap on the reservoir (no drag) fires that color's highest currently-affordable tier — here, Stone Wall — as the casual-flow shortcut. The Memorize (✨) button is unaffected by this gesture; it only commits the buffered word into mana.

① Word Buffer (12 slots)

F · L · A · M · E spelled with red blocks → charging Red. Slots show drag-handle dots (top), a hovered slot reveals an × destroy button with tooltip, and one slot is lifted to demonstrate the drag state. Empty slots stay visible so the 12-slot cap is always legible.

② Energy Reservoirs (color-blind safe)

Each reservoir is labelled by icon + color + name (🔥 / 🌿 / ❄️) so the three streams are distinguishable without color. Tick marks at T1·5 / T2·10 / T3·20 (red adds T4·40 for Volley) with cap at 60. Unlocked tiers glow gold; locked spells are dimmed in the roster strip below.

③ Memorize (commit button)

Icon-only ✨ button next to the buffer. Tap validates the buffered word against the dictionary. Valid → the word's energy transmutes into the matching color reservoirs (value-weighted), buffer clears. Invalid → fizzle: button shakes, buffer flashes red, and the buffer is cleared — the letters are consumed, so a bad commit costs the collected tiles. No spell fires from this action.

④ Cast (reservoir-driven)

Each color's reservoir is the cast surface for that color. Tap a reservoir = fire its highest currently-affordable tier. Drag from a reservoir = open a vertical menu listing all that color's affordable tiers (Firebolt/Fireball/Inferno/Volley for red), with locked tiers greyed; release on the desired tier to fire it. Release outside the menu cancels. See the Reservoir-cast active variant panel for the active state.
